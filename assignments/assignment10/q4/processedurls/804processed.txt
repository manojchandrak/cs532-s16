Justin Bieber’s First GQ Cover Is Here And Was So Worth The Wait

   WOW. WOW. WOW. WOW. WOW.
   by [8]Maeve Keirans 2/10/2016

   Please stop everything you’re doing. GQ just dropped [9]its March
   cover, and it features [10]Justin Bieber looking, like, so good. Like,
   a real life adult man, which makes sense since he’s covering THE
   gentleman’s magazine, y’know?
   Embedded from [11]www.instagram.com.

   IFRAME: [12]https://www.instagram.com/p/BBnKMw1RryC/embed/?taken-by=gq

   The cover — one of two — features a suited up Bieber, with his blond
   hair and sparse goatee front and center. While the full interview
   doesn’t drop until tomorrow, GQ has blessed us with [13]many photos
   from the shoot, which are more than enough to tide us over until then.
