Drake posts new album artwork around Toronto

   'Views From The 6' is due out in April
   [2015AppleMusic_Drake_GettyImages-476367588090615.article_x4.jpg] Getty
   [65]David Renshaw, 10th February 2016

Related Articles

     * [66]Drake claims 'Views' has sold 630,000 copies on its first day
     * [67]Drake explains why he cut Kanye and Jay Z from new album
       'Views'
     * [68]Drake releases new album 'Views' - stream/download it now

   A number of posters and billboards promoting [69]Drake's new album have
   emerged across Toronto overnight.
   The rapper will release 'Views From The 6' in April and a billboard
   with the word 'Views' illustrated by a series of eyeballs erected in
   the city centre.
   Meanwhile, other artwork and images have popped up at Billy Bishop
   Airport in the city. See both below, now:

   [70]All Star Weekend 2016

   A photo posted by champagnepapi (@champagnepapi) on Feb 9, 2016 at
   8:43pm PST

     The [71]#6god just landed at [72]@BBishopAirport. Welcome home.
     [73]#vft6 [74]pic.twitter.com/OEGBeNX8b4
     — Billy Bishop Airport (@BBishopAirport) [75]February 9, 2016

   Drake, who splits his time between Los Angeles and Toronto, will return
   to Canada to host this year's NBA All Star Weekend. He is expected to
   take part in an all-star game on a team alongside Win Butler of Arcade
   Fire.
   'Views From The 6' will be the follow up to Drake's 2015 mixtape 'If
   You're Reading This It's Too Late' and is set to feature [76]new single
   'Summer Sixteen'
   The news comesafter [77]Rihanna [78]dropped her new single 'Work',
   which features Drake. The Bajan singer [79]released her long-awaited
   album 'Anti' the following day.
