HBO Promotes Longtime Network Exec Nancy Lesser

   [21]The Hollywood Reporter

   February 10, 2016

   Nancy Lesser is moving up at HBO.

   The premium cable channel has promoted the network veteran to executive
   vp media and talent relations. As part of her new role, she now will
   oversee all program publicity efforts for HBO's drama and comedy
   series, original movies, miniseries and specials.

   Lesser, who most recently held the position of senior vp media and
   talent relations, has been at the pay cabler for 31 years. She started
   in the New York offices before she moved to Los Angeles to run the West
   Coast media relations department, later adding oversight of the talent
   relations department to her responsibilities.

   The news of the promotion comes two weeks after an executive shuffle at
   the network that saw drama head Michael Ellenberg exit and comedy exec
   Casey Bloys' purview expanded to include all HBO series, late night and
   specials.

   Lesser will continue to report to executive vp corporate communications
   Quentin Schaffer.
