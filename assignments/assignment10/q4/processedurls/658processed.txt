   (AllHipHop News) Kanye West is allowing his fans across the world to
   experience the Yeezy Season 3 fashion event in NYC. The show will
   stream live on the entertainment platform Tidal on Thursday, February
   11.

   According to reports, the Madison Square Garden showcase will also
   premiere Ye’s latest album T.L.O.P. (formerly So Help Me God, Swish,
   and Waves). The Yeezy Season 3 live stream can viewed [40]here at 4 pm
   ET.

     YEEZY Season 3. 2/11 4PM ET. [41]@TheGarden. Livestream for ALL on
     TIDAL. [42]https://t.co/flkoN5t26q

     — KANYE WEST (@kanyewest) [43]February 10, 2016
