   LOS ANGELES — Sex educator, author, speaker and XBIZ Premiere columnist
   Lynn Brown Rosenberg is featured in the article [59]“We’ve Come a Long
   Way, Baby! Sage Advice From Successful Women,” that appears in the
   HuffPost Women section of HuffingtonPost.com.

   “Today's women are breaking through the glass ceiling, taking on
   male-dominated roles, and venturing into uncharted territory,” Gray
   says in the article’s intro. “Here are stories and advice from six
   amazing women at the top of the game, running businesses and making new
   rules.”

   Rosenberg, who also serves as a health advisor for The Sinclaire
   Institute was among the speakers of last month’s Sexual Health Expo in
   Los Angeles, where she offered sex advice to baby boomers and adults
   over the age of 50.
   [60]Sexual Health Expo - Sep 24-25, New York City

    In the HuffPost article, she is profiled alongside female business
   owners and executives.

    "Success is ageless,” Rosenberg told the Huffington Post. “My journey
   as a woman encompasses a wide range of careers (professional
   pianist/vocalist, screenwriter, entrepreneur, memoir author, crime
   novelist). None have been as interesting as the journey I'm on now.
   Sharing intimate details of my journey to sexual and emotional freedom
   in ‘My Sexual Awakening at 70’ has been a triumph for me, and has
   resulted in others of all ages making shifts in their lives to express
   and receive greater joy."

   Rosenberg told XBIZ, "I am excited and proud to be recognized by the
   HuffPost as a successful woman. I am living my truth and my dreams and
   to be acknowledged like this just adds to my joy."
