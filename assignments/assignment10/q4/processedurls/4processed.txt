   2/10/2016

EBMC Residential Manager Re-Elected to Local NARPM Chapter

   EBMC Residential Property Manager Tina Morris was recently re-elected
   as Secretary to the Board of Directors for the [106]Northern Nevada
   Chapter of National Association of Residential Property Managers
   (NARPM). She has been an active member of NARPM since May 2014.

   NARPM provides a place for Property Mangers to talk with other Property
   Mangers in your area through local monthly events. NARPM promotes a
   high standard of business ethics, professionalism and fair housing
   practices through continuing professional education classes in property
   management.

   Tina started at EBMC in March, 2009. Working from EBMC's Reno office,
   she manages a portfolio of 88 units, comprised of small apartment
   communities, condominiums, and single family homes.

   Tina's role as Secretary for NARPM is indicative of EBMC's industry
   participation efforts. We are proud of her accomplishment and service.

   Founded in 1968, Eugene Burger Management Corporation is a full service
   real estate management firm with regional offices throughout California
   and Nevada. EBMC is an Accredited Management Organization (AMO) by the
   institute of Real Estate Management. EBMC specializes in Commercial,
   Residential and Association Management and Leasing.
