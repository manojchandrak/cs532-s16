Report: Ex-NFLer Josh Morgan charged with misdemeanor reckless use of a
firearm

   by [4]Arun Srinivasan February 10, 2016 11:59pm
   [cropped_REU_2299752.jpg?ts=1455148391]
   [cropped_REU_2299752.jpg?ts=1455148391]
   Jeff Hanisch / USA TODAY Sports

   Former NFL wide receiver Josh Morgan was charged with misdemeanor
   reckless use of a firearm after accidentally shooting himself,
   according to [5]TMZ Sports.

   Morgan reportedly was cleaning the gun when it fired on Jan. 31, per
   the report.

   Police were called to Morgan's Virginia residence after responding to a
   report of a self-inflicted gunshot wound.

   Morgan will reportedly need to appear in court in March.

   The 30-year-old signed with the [6]New Orleans Saints but was released
   prior to the 2015 season. Morgan also played for the [7]San Francisco
   49ers, [8]Washington Redskins, and [9]Chicago Bears.
