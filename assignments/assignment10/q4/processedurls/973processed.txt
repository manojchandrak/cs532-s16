McFarlane Toys Announces Titanfall 2 Line

   [72]News
   by [73]Matt Bertz on February 10, 2016 at 06:20 PM
   7,509 Views
   [74]6

   It appears Electronic Arts and Respawn Entertainment are thinking big
   with the second installment in the Titanfall series.

   A week after Forbes [75]published a report claiming Titanfall 2 will
   include a dedicated single-player campaign and a television spinoff,
   today McFarlane Toys [76]announced plans for a line of Titanfall 2
   themed toys. The press release says these toys are a part of the "new
   2016 collector box program," stand seven inches tall, and that they are
   slated for a winter 2016 release "in conjunction with the highly
   anticipated launch of Titanfall 2."

   To learn more about how Respawn designed the original Titans, watch
   [77]Ben Hanson's video.


   Our Take
   The winter 2016 release window for these figures is interesting
   considering EA's previous statement only pointed to a game launch
   before March 2017. If Respawn accelerates development it could make for
   a crowded holiday 2016 shooter space, with Gears of War 4, the next
   Infinity Ward Call of Duty title, and EA's own Battlefield 5 all
   currently slated to join the fray. However, EA and Respawn will
   ultimately determine when the game launches, whether or not the toy
   line is ready.
