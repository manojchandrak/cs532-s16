Travelore News: Meliá Hotels International Introduces Its First Property In
Jamaica, The All-Inclusive Meliá Braco Village in Rio Bueno

                           Logo del Meliá Atlanta

           Largest Spanish Hotel Company Opens Second Hotel in the
            English-speaking Caribbean, and Its First in Jamaica

    [5]Meliá Hotels International, one of the largest hotel companies in
   the world, announced today the opening of the all-inclusive [6]Meliá
   Braco Village in Rio Bueno, Jamaica as part of its [7]Meliá Hotels &
   Resorts brand. This highly anticipated opening is the company’s second
   hotel in the English-speaking Caribbean after [8]Meliá Nassau Beach in
   the Bahamas, and it’s first in Jamaica.
   With a total of 226 spacious guest rooms, including 61 suites, the
   all-inclusive Meliá Braco Village features a choice of six restaurants,
   three bars and two exquisite swimming pools. Guests can choose from a
   variety of garden view and oceanfront rooms equipped with plush
   bathrobes and slippers, Nespresso coffee machines, minibar, iron, 42
   inch Flat Screen TV, high speed Wi-Fi internet and 24 hour room
   service.
   The resort offers an exceptional gourmet experience with six on-site
   restaurants including The Market Place, an open-kitchen concept
   featuring an international buffet; 876 PRIME serving up steaks and
   chops; O’Grille, a beachside Jamaican restaurant that incorporates
   traditional Caribbean dishes made with authentic, locally sourced
   ingredients; Rasta Pasta, an a-la-carte Italian restaurant; Nikkei, an
   innovative culinary concept featuring Japanese and Peruvian fusion
   cuisine served at Teppanyaki style tables; and Golden Sand, serving as
   the property’s beachside restaurant. In addition to dining options,
   Meliá Braco Village will have three distinct bars, each offering a
   different atmosphere: Jigger is the hotel’s piano bar that is perfect
   for a quiet, intimate drink; The Shaker, complete with a curated DJ
   experience complete with modern tropical cocktails in a vibrant seaside
   setting; and Chill Out Bar, which offers traditional jerk style roasted
   cuisine during the daytime and a relaxing cocktail experience at night.
   For the discerning traveler, Meliá Braco Village offers Meliá Hotels &
   Resorts’ signature concept, [9]THE LEVEL, complete with a VIP Level
   Lounge featuring private check-in service and light bites in the
   afternoon; the beachside Golden Sand restaurant serving breakfast,
   lunch and dinner daily; exclusive designated areas for families,
   couples and groups; personalized concierge service; top-category rooms;
   and premium alcohol service. Room choices include breathtaking
   oceanfront rooms and decadent beachside rooms, each option replete with
   magnificent views. THE LEVEL lounge boasts floor to ceiling windows
   with breathtaking ocean views, as well as its own YHI spa treatment
   suites as part of the lounge, a first for Meliá Hotels and Resorts
   brand. In addition, guests of THE LEVEL are treated to the ultimate
   pampering experience with complimentary sunscreen and cooling Aloe Vera
   lotion at a dedicated pool and daily turndown service. Amenities
   provided at THE LEVEL offer guests the perfect hotel within a hotel
   experience.
   During the day, all guests have access to two dazzling pools, as well
   as an array of activities. A variety of restoring spa treatments are
   offered in the signature [10]YHI Spa with 8 massage rooms available,
   and an onsite fitness center is open 24 hours a day so guests can
   workout at their leisure. Guests can also engage in a friendly sports
   match at the resort’s basketball court or football pitch, perfect for
   families and groups.
   Families are catered to with a wide assortment of specialty services
   including a babysitting service and Meliá Hotels & Resorts’ [11]Kids &
   Co.concept for children ages five to 12. Melia Braco Village’s youngest
   guests can partake in a range of interactive games, arts and crafts
   projects and more.
   For the business traveler, Meliá Braco Village offers a business
   center, Wi-Fi in public spaces, and two designated rooms for meetings
   and events with capacity for up to 100 people. The hotel’s two intimate
   meeting venues are perfect for small to medium-size groups, and there
   is a variety of outdoor and indoor public spaces for all types of
   events and special occasions.
   A picturesque destination for weddings and special events, Meliá Braco
   Village offers a multitude of romance experiences for guests. Two
   gazebos, one overlooking the ocean and one in the property’s lush
   garden area, are the ideal setting for couple to exchange their vows,
   along with a variety of indoor options as well. For more information
   about Melia Hotels & Resorts’ romance offerings,
   visit [12]www.romancebymelia.com.
   Starting rates for Meliá Braco Village are $200/per person per night.
   For more information please visit [13]www.melia.com.
