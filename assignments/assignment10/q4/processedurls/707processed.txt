Got News?

Most news organizations rely on solid tips from people in touch with the
local scene to develop stories. If you see news happening, or have a tip,
please give our newsroom a call at 860-887-3511. Or, send us an e-mail
at [65]news@wich.com. All sources will be kept confidential.

Got Events?

If you have a civic function or a school event you’d like us to promote on
the air, feel free to let us know about it. We’re always trying to keep
listeners up-to-date on what’s happening in the community, and you can help
us spread the word. [66]news@wich.com
