<p><a href="http://www.okayplayer.com/news/maurice-white-dead-age-74.html">As we all know at this point</a>, last week we sadly lost the legendary <strong>Maurice White</strong> to a long bout with Parkinsons. The impact his music had on my youth through <strong>Earth Wind &amp; Fire</strong> can’t be overstated (“Reasons” will always be one of my favorite songs). Though he has sadly passed, Maurice’s legacy will forever live on through his music. <strong><a href="http://www.gillespetersonworldwide.com/in-memory-of-maurice-white-of-earth-wind-fire-mix/" target="_blank">Gilles Peterson</a></strong> pays tribute to Maurice with this amazing 2 hour plus 37 song mix, <i>In Memory of Maurice White</i>.</p>
<blockquote><p><span style="color: #808080;">I avoided the really obvious hits that you can hear everywhere and went for my personal faves, and a solid dose of the music that went on to shape the unique sound of Earth Wind and Fire from his early years working with two great mentors to him, Ramsey Lewis and Charles Stepney.</span></p></blockquote>
