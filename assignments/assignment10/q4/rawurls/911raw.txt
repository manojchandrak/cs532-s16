<p>MANILA - The latest episode of &quot;On the Wings of Love&quot; had fans pleading to the series&#39; writers, &quot;quit playing games with our hearts, you&#39;re tearing us apart.&quot;</p>

<p>If you&#39;re anything like the thousands of fans online, then you&#39;re probably reeling from the series&#39; February 10 episode aptly hashtagged &quot;#OTWOLTheBreakUp.&quot;</p>

<p>It picked up where the last one left off, with Clark and Leah struggling to make their long-distance relationship work. An increasingly frustrated Clark battles for the scraps of attention he gets from Leah, whose focus remains her job.</p>

<p>It wasn&#39;t all dark and gloomy, however, as the episode also sprinkled &quot;kilig&quot; moments via characters such as Tatang and Nanang, Tiffany and Tolayts, and Jigs and Angela.</p>

<p>Fast forward to six months after and Leah found herself back in the Philippines to personally support her father&#39;s heart transplant. The surgery was a success, but being back home meant that she will have to face Clark, who --at this point-- was suffering from a bad case of inferiority complex.</p>

<p>Clark confronted Leah, insisting that she gives up her work in Dubai and stay with him. He puts forth several arguments to make her do so such as her boss is in love with her and that she was turning into someone who would readily sacrifice family for her career.</p>

<p>He conveniently dodged replies from Leah, who was trying to rescue their relationship by hammering down the point that she didn&#39;t care whether her boss was in love with her --the important things was that she was in love with him.</p>

<p>Clark, unusually stubborn, ignored Leah&#39;s pleas and threw at her words such as &quot;I don&#39;t know you anymore&quot; and &quot;You&#39;re only human, puwede ka madala ng tukso.&quot;</p>

<p>Fuming, Leah slapped Clark. &quot;Issue ito ng trust,&quot; she shouted at her husband. &quot;Wala kang tiwala sa akin. ...Trabaho lang iyon. Walang ibig sabihin iyon. Ilang beses ko bang sasabihin sa iyo iyon?&quot;</p>

<p>Clark, seemingly adamant to break up with Leah, began forcing his wife to admit that she would rather work in Dubai than live with him. Apparently, that has always been his problem with Leah&#39;s decision all along.</p>

<p>&quot;Hindi na kita kilala! Look at yourself! Iba ka na! Iba magsalita, iba tumayo, at iba na rin ang priorities mo --hindi na ako!&quot; Clark lectured Leah, before issuing an ultimatum. &quot;If you go back to Dubai, tuluyan nang masisira ang relationship natin. So it&#39;s time for you to choose: it&#39;s me or your career.&quot;</p>

<p>To which Leah, who did not want to reply at first, answered: &quot;Trabaho ko.&quot;</p>
