<p><strong>(AllHipHop News)</strong> Kanye West is allowing his fans across the world to experience the <em>Yeezy Season 3 </em>fashion event in NYC. The show will stream live on the entertainment platform Tidal on <span class="aBn"><span class="aQJ">Thursday, February 11. </span></span></p>
<p>According to reports, the Madison Square Garden showcase will also premiere Ye&#8217;s latest album <em>T.L.O.P. </em>(formerly <em>So Help Me God</em>,<em> Swish</em>, and <em>Waves</em>). The<em> Yeezy Season 3</em> live stream can viewed <a href="http://tidal.com/us/yeezyseason3">here</a> at 4 pm ET.</p>
