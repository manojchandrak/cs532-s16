<p>Ten years ago today, <a href="http://pitchfork.com/artists/2248-j-dilla/">J Dilla</a> <a href="http://pitchfork.com/news/24579-jay-dee-dead-at-32/">passed away</a> at the age of 32. That same day, Flying Lotus spun a live mix for <a href="http://dublab.com/flying-lotus-dillas-still-here-mix-02-10-06/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">dublab</a>, dedicating it to Dilla. Today, 10 years later, dublab has shared that mix. The hour-long mix includes some of Dilla's songs, as well as tracks that Dilla sampled for his own music. Listen to Flying Lotus' 2006 "J Dilla Mix" <a href="http://dublab.com/flying-lotus-dillas-still-here-mix-02-10-06/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">here</a>.</p>

<p>Last year, Dilla's mother (Ma Dukes) and Vintage Vibez Music Group released <a href="http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/21156-dillatronic/"><i>Dillatronic</i></a>, a 41-track album of rare instrumentals.</p>
<p>In other recent FlyLo news, the producer is included on Rick Rubin's upcoming <i>Star Wars</i>-themed album, <i><a href="http://pitchfork.com/news/63412-star-wars-album-to-feature-flying-lotus-rick-rubin-jj-abrams-listen-to-new-flying-lotus-rubin-and-baauer-tracks/">Star Wars Headspace</a></i>.</p>
