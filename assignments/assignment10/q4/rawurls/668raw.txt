data-redactor-tag="em" data-verified="redactor">The Atelier Versace spring/summer 2016 finale</em></p><p
class="body-el-text standard-body-el-text">The Versace Medusa &ndash; the fashion house's&nbsp;iconic symbol&nbsp;&ndash; has been given the emoji treatment just in time for Valentine's Day.&nbsp;</p><p
class="body-el-text standard-body-el-text">The brand's&nbsp;new free emoji&nbsp;app,&nbsp;which is available for both iOS and Android,&nbsp;launches on 14&nbsp;February&nbsp;and can be used to embellish photos or to create original images using a wide range of emoji stickers, backgrounds, filters and text-colour options.</p><div
class="ad-gpt-sharethrough"><div
class="gpt-tag" id="ad-gpt-sharethrough"></div></div><p
class="body-el-text standard-body-el-text"><span
lang="EN-US" data-redactor-tag="span" data-verified="redactor">"</span>I love how the new Versace emoji app lets everyone in the world express themselves and share their feelings,<span
lang="EN-US" data-redactor-tag="span" data-verified="redactor">"</span> Donatella Versace said of the launch. <span
lang="EN-US" data-redactor-tag="span" data-verified="redactor">"</span>To have our own Versace emoji is a real treat for Valentine<span
lang="EN-US" data-redactor-tag="span" data-verified="redactor">'</span>s Day, and I can<span
lang="EN-US" data-redactor-tag="span" data-verified="redactor">'</span>t wait to begin creating with it.<span
lang="EN-US" data-redactor-tag="span" data-verified="redactor">"</span></p><p
class="body-el-text standard-body-el-text">To celebrate the new app, the house has also created a capsule collection of Versace emoji T-shirts, printed with the Medusa emoji either&nbsp;wearing sunglasses or sporting hearts for eyes. The T-shirts come in both black and white and will be available online and in Versace boutiques worldwide come 14 February.</p><div
