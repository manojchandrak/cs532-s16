	<div itemprop="articlebody" class="entry-content">
		<p>TV&#8217;s <a  href="http://tvline.com/tag/marvels-agent-carter"  >Agent Carter</a> is looking to moonlight, on a very different kind of mission.<span id="more-682627"></span></p>
<p><a  href="http://tvline.com/tag/hayley-atwell/"  >Hayley Atwell</a> has landed the lead in <em>Conviction</em>, a drama pilot in development at ABC.</p>
<p class="pmc-related-link related">
			<strong class="pmc-related-type">RELATED</strong><a target="_self" href="http://tvline.com/2016/01/20/pilot-season-abc-cbs-cw-fox-nbc-2016-2017/" title="Pilot Season &#8217;16: Scoop on This Fall&#8217;s (Possible) New Shows and Stars">Pilot Season &#8217;16: Scoop on This Fall&#8217;s (Possible) New Shows and Stars</a>
		</p>
<p>The project will star Atwell as the brilliant-but-rebellious daughter of a Clinton-like political family, who is forced into taking a job as the head of Los Angeles’ newly created Conviction Integrity Unit. Along with her team of lawyers, investigators and forensic experts, she has two weeks to examine cases where there&#8217;s credible suspicion that the wrong person may have been convicted of a crime.</p>
<p class="pmc-related-link related">
			<strong class="pmc-related-type">RELATED</strong><a target="_self" href="http://tvline.com/2016/02/10/the-flash-earth-two-episode-ratings/" title="Tuesday Ratings: &lt;i&gt;Agent Carter&lt;/i&gt; Stabilizes in Week 4">Tuesday Ratings: <i>Agent Carter</i> Stabilizes in Week 4</a>
		</p>
<p><strong>What does this mean for <em>Agent Carter</em>? </strong>In the event that <em>Conviction</em> is ordered to series <em>and</em> ABC&#8217;s Marvel drama is renewed for Season 3 (after returning to depressed ratings), TVLine hears that Atwell&#8217;s schedule would be worked out so that she could do <em>both</em> roles.</p>
<p>Mark Gordon and Nick Pepper will executive produce the pilot with co-creators Liz Friedman and Liz Friedlander. Friedman wrote the pilot, and Friedlander will direct.</p>
