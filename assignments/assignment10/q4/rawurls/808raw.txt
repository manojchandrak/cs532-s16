<p>Tesla is set to unveil the production Model 3 electric vehicle on March 31, according to its quarterly earnings release. Tesla's aggressive pricing puts the Model 3 slightly lower than the upcoming Bolt, which Chevy prices at $37,500 MSRP. </p><p>But, you'll have to wait a while to purchase a Model 3. Production and delivery of the budget EV doesn't start until late 2017.
