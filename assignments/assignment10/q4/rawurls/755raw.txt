	<h1>Uncharted 4 Mo-Cap Restarted, Bad News for Development?</h1>
	
	<div class="meta">
				<span class="author">By <a href="http://segmentnext.com/author/sikander/" title="Posts by Sikandar Mahmood" rel="author">Sikandar Mahmood</a> &nbsp; / &nbsp; 3 months ago </span>

	<div class="cover"><img width="620" height="349" src="http://cdn.segmentnext.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/uncharted-4-multiplayer-6-620x349.jpg" class="attachment-medium size-medium wp-post-image" alt="Uncharted 4 Copies" /></div>	

			
	<div class="body">

			
		
				
		<p>Uncharted 4 mo-cap shoot wrapped up last October and then all developers needed to do was get the game ready for release in the coming months. At least that&#8217;s what we thought. However, it looks like despite Neil Druckmann&#8217;s announcement of a wrap, Mo-Cap has restarted.</p>
<p><center><br />
<blockquote class="twitter-tweet" data-lang="en">
<p lang="en" dir="ltr">That&#39;s a mocap wrap for Nathan Drake. Take a bow, <a href="https://twitter.com/nolan_north">@nolan_north</a>, you&#39;ve earned it. <a href="http://t.co/W4CZUKAYEC">pic.twitter.com/W4CZUKAYEC</a></p>
<p>&mdash; Neil Druckmann (@Neil_Druckmann) <a href="https://twitter.com/Neil_Druckmann/status/655172166166536192">October 17, 2015</a></p></blockquote>
<p><script async src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js" charset="utf-8"></script></center></p>
<p>Here&#8217;s a Tweet from Jermy Yates:</p>
<p><center><br />
<blockquote class="twitter-tweet" data-lang="en">
<p lang="en" dir="ltr">Donning the Drake suit just ONE LAST TIME. <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/Uncharted4?src=hash">#Uncharted4</a> <a href="https://t.co/g5FOzzUJko">pic.twitter.com/g5FOzzUJko</a></p>
<p>&mdash; Jeremy Yates (@Jeremy_Yates) <a href="https://twitter.com/Jeremy_Yates/status/696801015862071296">February 8, 2016</a></p></blockquote>
<p><script async src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js" charset="utf-8"></script></center></p>
<p>This could mean a number of things. </p>
<p>Developers may not be happy with certain scenes and wanted to redo them, which isn&#8217;t good as the game is so close to release and has already been delayed twice. On the other hand, could there be a story DLC is the works? The second possibility is a stretch but it is possible. </p>
<p>May be after the mo-cap ended for the game a part of the development team started working on a DLC? We can&#8217;t be sure but we really hope that this won&#8217;t cause another delay.</p>
<p>Uncharted 4 was first pushed from 2015 to March 2016, and will <a href="http://segmentnext.com/2015/12/23/uncharted-4-delayed-again-naughty-dog-needs-more-time/" target="_blank">now debut in April</a>. The game will release exclusively on PlayStation 4.</p>
