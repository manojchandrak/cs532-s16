				 <p>AUSTIN, Texas – The Texas soccer program received some impressive news on Wednesday by learning that its high-profile, nine-member 2016 signing class had been ranked as both the top class in the Big 12 Conference and as the No. 15 class in the country according to a release by Top Drawer Soccer.</p><p><p>The Longhorns' program added nine signees, including seven freshmen and a pair of standout goalkeeping transfers, on last week's National Signing Day.</p><p><p>Signees included (Hometown/High School or last College/Club Soccer Team): forward <strong>Cydney Billups</strong> (Greenwood Village, Colo./Mountain Vista High School/Colorado Rush & Real Colorado), goalkeeper <strong>Taylor Braun</strong> (Colleyville, Texas/transfer from the University of Kentucky/Dallas Texans Red), goalkeeper <strong>Paige Brown</strong> (Jackson, Mo./transfer from the University of Louisville/St. Louis Scott Gallagher), goalkeeper <strong>Nicole Curry</strong> (Dallas, Texas/The Episcopal School of Dallas/Dallas Sting ECNL 98), forward <strong>Kyra Falcone</strong> (San Antonio, Texas/Douglas MacArthur High School/Classics Elite 98 Premier), forward <strong>Cyera Hintzen</strong> (McKinney, Texas/Sachse High School/Dallas Sting ECNL 98), defender <strong>Emily Martin</strong> (Las Vegas, Nev./Bishop Gorman High School/Heat FC ECNL 98), defender <strong>Atu Mshana</strong> (Garland, Texas/Bishop Lynch High School/Dallas Texans ECNL) and forward <strong>Tecora Turner</strong> (Memphis, Tenn./St. Agnes Academy/98 Lady Lobos Rush Premier & FC Alliance 98).</p><p><p>The Top Drawer Soccer Top 20 Recruiting Classes in order included: 1. UCLA, 2. Virginia, 3. North Carolina, 4. Duke, 5. Boston College, 6. Stanford, 7. USC, 8. Florida State, 9. Kentucky, 10. South Carolina, 11. Santa Clara, 12. Rutgers, 13. Clemson, 14. Utah, <strong><u>15. TEXAS</u></strong>, 16. Vanderbilt, 17. Miami (FL), 18. Syracuse, 19. Penn State, 20. Tennessee.</p><p><p>To read all about the 2016 Texas soccer recruiting class, please visit this <a href="http://texassports.com/news/2016/2/8/WSOC_0208165213.aspx">LINK</a>.</p><div class='print-friendly-story-button'><a onclick='window.open(this.href,"_blank");return false;' href='/news/2016/2/10/WSOC_0210165400.aspx?print=true'>Print Friendly Version</a></div>
