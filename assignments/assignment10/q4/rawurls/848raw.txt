Most of the time stupid characters are simply sidekicks meant to make the hero look smart by comparison.<br/>
<br/>
Sometimes, however, on rare occasions, stupid people can be done so well that the audience will follow them wherever they go. They achieve a level of brilliance within their own idiocy. But creating a truly beloved moron is no simple task. It&rsquo;s so easy for a character to be as annoying to the audience as they are to the other people on screen.
