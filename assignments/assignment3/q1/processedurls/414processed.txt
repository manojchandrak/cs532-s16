TSMC will be sole supplier of processors for next iPhones - Electronic Times

     * Posted 11 Feb 2016 08:10

     *





     Email

     More
     *
     *
          + [42]A
          + [43]A
     *

   SEOUL: Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Co Ltd (TSMC) will be the
   exclusive supplier of mobile processors for Apple Inc's next iPhones,
   South Korea's Electronic Times reported on Thursday citing unnamed
   industry sources.

   TSMC beat rival chipmaker Samsung Electronics Co Ltd to make the
   processors using 10-nanometre manufacturing technology, the report
   said. Samsung and TSMC both supply the processors for the iPhone 6S and
   iPhone 6S Plus.

   (Reporting by Se Young Lee; Editing by Michael Perry)

   - Reuters
