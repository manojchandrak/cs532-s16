   Sort-of-Doctors Peter Capaldi and John Hurt lend their support to the
   junior doctors' strike

   By Huw Fullerton
   Wednesday 10 February 2016 at 9:23AM

   With cuts facing the UK’s junior physicians and a strike taking place
   today to protest Health Secretary Jeremy Hunt’s plans for more weekend
   working at functionally lower pay, it's time our Doctors stood together
   – and apparently that even includes the time-travelling variety.

   Yes, Time Lords John Hurt and Peter Capaldi (the War Doctor and the
   current Doctor, respectively) have thrown their weight behind the
   junior doctors, donning Vivienne Westwood-designed T-shirts as part of
   a campaign designed to support the NHS workers.
