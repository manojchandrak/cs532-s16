Waterstones children’s book prize 2016 shortlists - in pictures

   Discover the shortlists for the three categories of the Waterstones
   children’s book prize 2016 - illustrated books, younger fiction, older
   fiction - where six books compete within each category to be crowned
   category winner, with the three category winners then vying for the
   overall title of Waterstones Children’s Book of the Year 2016. The
   winners will be announced on Thursday 17 March

   [38]Michelle Pauli
