UX Integrator

   [24]Apply

   Job Description:

   Our client is seeking a UX Integrator.

   Summary:

   -We are a UX team in the Research and Machine Intelligence group. We
   are working on multiple, unreleased products that use machine learning
   to solve complex problems and are quickly moving from conceptual phases
   of development to detailed execution.

   -The UX Integrator works closely with both UX designers and engineers
   on our product teams to ensure that the experience of the services we
   are building are precise. They bring the designs to life with motion
   and refined interaction.

   -The ideal candidate will be a software engineer with an eye for detail
   and product excellence. They can manage their own workload across
   multiple projects. They can reconcile the technical constraints of a
   complex build with both the demands of learning new technologies and
   the goals of setting a high bar for user-experience.

   Responsibilities:

   -Work with UX design team to evolve and implement UI designs

   -Build out prototype UIs for evaluation by both product team and
   user-research

   -Work with SWE team to integrate UI code into builds quickly and
   efficiently

   Minimum Requirements:

   -Bachelor's degree

   -2+ years experience as a UI-focussed SW engineer

   -Java/Java Script experience

   -Strong communications skills

   -Prior UI template work for Android.

   -Android UI framework experience

   Preferred Requirements:

   -2+ years experience shipping Android apps

   -Worked as an engineer in a design-focused environment

   *Portfolio that highlights a high level of craft must be on the resume
