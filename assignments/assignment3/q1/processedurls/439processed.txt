About the Job

   Hello There,
   My name is Tanuja working with Max Eleven. We have a contract position
   open for SDET in Seattle, WA. Please find the JD mentioned below and If
   you are available and interested please do send me your updated profile
   in a word document.

   Role: JavaScript SDET

   Location: Seattle, WA

   Duration: 3 Year Project

   Responsibilities:
   Analyze existing functionality and tests
   Understanding of enterprise application development
   Creation and review of test cases
   Troubleshooting, debugging, and analysis
   Self-motivated, proactive, curious, responsible and flexible
   Required:
   5+ years of enterprise software testing experience for high traffic
   applications
   Experience being a member of an enterprise software development team
   Experience with continuous delivery
   Strong experience with Selenium web testing
   Experience testing RESTful APIs
   Strong experience with JavaScript
   Strong experience with unit testing JavaScript (QUnit, Mocha, etc.)
   Desired:
   Experience with JUnit
   Experience with object-oriented development
   Experience with relational databases (SQL Server)
   Experience with non-relational databases (MongoDB)
   Experience in an agile development team
   Experience with Python
   Experience with Java
   Experience with cloud services (AWS, Azure, etc.)
