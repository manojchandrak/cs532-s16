INNER SANCTUM | Auckland Nines Day One

   Wed 10th February, 05:00PM
   A A

   Step inside the sheds, onto the sideline and into the huddle with the
   Dyldam Parramatta Eels on day one of their Auckland Nines Campaign.

   Hear Head Coach Brad Arthur give his halftime talk, see the boys
   prepare pre-game, and hear the reactions of the Blue and Gold coaching
   staff as the Eels first two pool games unfold. From the dusty first-up
   loss to the Storm, bouncing back against the Roosters to stay alive in
   the competition, experience every moment with the team.

   Tomorrow: follow the Eels from their final pool game to the semi final
   as they win their fourth back-to-back game to earn a place in the Grand
   Final decider.

   Click through to see the highlights from the Eels' first [116]Waiheke
   Pool clash against the Melbourne Storm, and their [117]second match
   against the Sydney Roosters.
