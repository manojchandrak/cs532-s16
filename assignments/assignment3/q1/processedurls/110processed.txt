   Are there more victims than meets the eye on Wednesday's [56]Criminal
   Minds?

   After a woman escapes a home where she's been held captive for years
   with two other women, the BAU is focused on catching the unsub... until
   JJ ([57]A.J. Cook) points out one of the other victims' odd behavior.

   "Violet [Amelia Rose Blaire] could not wait to get out of here," she
   says in the exclusive sneak peek above. "It's like she had somewhere
   specific in mind. ... What if she has a child? Where else would you
   wanna go so badly?"

   [58]Winter TV: Must-see new shows

   "That's not a stretch. Sheila Woods [Pearl Dickson] had a miscarriage,"
   Rossi ([59]Joe Mantegna) says, referring to another victim. "Maybe
   Violet brought one to term."

   So, where is this other kid? Watch the clip above.

   Criminal Minds airs Wednesdays at 9/8c on CBS.
