Listen to Flying Lotus' Mix From the Day J Dilla Passed Away

The iconic Detroit producer/rapper passed away 10 years ago today

By [107]


    Matthew Strauss

on February 10, 2016 at 6:49 p.m. EST

   Listen to Flying Lotus' Mix From the Day J Dilla Passed Away

   Flying Lotus photo by [108]Erez Avissar

   Ten years ago today, [109]J Dilla [110]passed away at the age of 32.
   That same day, Flying Lotus spun a live mix for [111]dublab, dedicating
   it to Dilla. Today, 10 years later, dublab has shared that mix. The
   hour-long mix includes some of Dilla's songs, as well as tracks that
   Dilla sampled for his own music. Listen to Flying Lotus' 2006 "J Dilla
   Mix" [112]here.

   Last year, Dilla's mother (Ma Dukes) and Vintage Vibez Music Group
   released [113]Dillatronic, a 41-track album of rare instrumentals.

   In other recent FlyLo news, the producer is included on Rick Rubin's
   upcoming Star Wars-themed album, [114]Star Wars Headspace.

   Read our [115]interview with Flying Lotus.

   Watch Flying Lotus' episode of "Over/Under":
