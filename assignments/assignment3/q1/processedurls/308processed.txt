Lead iOS Engineer

   [24]Apply

   zulily is looking for talented engineers with a desire to build amazing
   software. Our team is small and you'll play a key role in shaping our
   product and our culture. We iterate quickly and strive to deliver the
   best user experience possible.

   We are a close-knit team of iOS, Android, and web service developers,
   designers and product managers collaborating to make zulily’s mobile
   applications incredible. We work in a high-speed, agile, continuous
   delivery environment to ship valuable features in the shortest time
   possible.


   YOU HAVE:

   ·       A strong background with iOS development

   ·       Proficiency with the Apple and iOS ecosystems

   ·       Shipped at least one iOS app

   ·       Passion for user interface and user experience

   ·       Experience optimizing mobile applications

   ·       Experience testing your own work

   ·       Experience mentoring other iOS Developers

   ·       Raised the bar in previous positions

   Bonus points for:

   ·       Experience in retail / eCommerce

   ·       Experience working with web services

   ·       Sample apps or open source code

   WHY zulily?

   At zulily you'll be working on native apps that reach over 1.5 million
   active users per month.

   You own your code at zulily. There is no QA team – everyone is
   responsible for quality.

   All technology employees get a Hack Week – a week of paid time off to
   hack on a project.

   We push to the app store frequently, striving for weekly releases. We
   move very fast.

   We are innovative and metric-driven, with a team passionate for
   delighting our customers.

   We have very high standards when it comes to hiring, and we're not
   compromising. As a result, you'll work with incredibly talented people,
   solving really hard problems, and we think that's fun.

   LINKs

   Engineering blog - [25]http://engineering.zulily.com

About Zulily

   About Zulily
   zulily is a retailer obsessed with bringing our customers something
   special every day. Unique products from up-and-coming brands are
   featured alongside favorites from top brands, including clothing, home
   decor, accessories, toys and gifts, giving customers something new to
   discover each morning at an incredible price. We are fast-paced,
   innovative and metric-driven, with a team passionate for delighting our
   customers. Whether you want to work with incredible product selection,
   develop smart marketing plans, help us in our pursuit of faster
   fulfillment, or do something else awesome in between, we have a place
   for you. With locations in Seattle, WA; Reno, NV; Columbus, OH and
   Bethlehem, PA, our team is excited to be changing the way people shop
   every day - and we are just getting started.
