Hockey: Level 1 Umpire Course

   We are pleased to announce that a Level 1 Umpire Course is being held
   at  Framlingham Hockey Club on Sunday 3rd April.  More details and to
   book can be found here >>
   http://englandhockey.co.uk/courses.asp?section=296&sectionTitle=Find+a+
   Course (currently page 2).

   Places are first come first served and only secure when booked and paid
   for online. Minimum participants is 14.

   Details of the course are as follows:

   Date: Sunday 3rd April 2016
   Time: 9am-4:40pm
   Venue: College Rd, Framlingham, Woodbridge IP13 9EY
   Course Format: Option 2

   Please be advised bookings must be made in the name of the participant
   â€“ bookings cannot be made on behalf of someone else. Guidance for those
   having trouble with the booking process is available at the link above.

   Before attending the course participants need to complete the online
   rules test via http://rules.englandhockey.co.uk/ (separate log in) and
   bring their certificate to the course.

   Please book early to avoid disappointment.

   Please send any queries to umpiring@englandhockey.co.uk
