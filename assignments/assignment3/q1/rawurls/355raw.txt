        <h1 class="header-title">iDevices Sells iGrill and Kitchen Thermometers to Weber</h1>
        <span class="byline">Wednesday February 10, 2016 1:00 PM PST by <a class="author-url" href="http://www.macrumors.com/author/joe-rossignol/">Joe Rossignol</a></span>
      </div>

      <div id="content" class="post">
        <div class="wrapper">
          <div class="body">iDevices has announced that its app-enabled iGrill and Kitchen Thermometer accessories have been acquired by <a href="http://www.weber.com/">Weber-Stephen Products</a>, a worldwide manufacturer of charcoal, gas, and electric outdoor grills and related accessories. The acquisition includes the iGrill2, iGrill mini, Kitchen Thermometer, and Kitchen Thermometer mini. The terms of the agreement are confidential.<br/>
<br/>
<img src="http://cdn.macrumors.com/article-new/2016/02/iGrill-Weber-iDevices.jpg" alt="iGrill-Weber-iDevices" width="550" height="450" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-488067" /><br/>
Weber will partner with iDevices to release a new iGrill app, which is expected to be available in spring 2016. Until then, iGrill and Kitchen Thermometer users can continue using their accessories through the existing <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/app/idevices-connected/id682656390?mt=8">iDevices Connected</a> app. Unlike some of its other products, iDevices's iGrill and Kitchen Thermometer accessories are not compatible with Apple's <a href="http://www.macrumors.com/guide/homekit-101-getting-started-beginners/">HomeKit</a> smart home platform.<br/>
<br/>
iDevices said it will now be able to focus more on becoming a leader in the home automation market. The accessory maker already sells <a href="http://store.idevicesinc.com/">multiple HomeKit-enabled products</a>, including a Switch, Outdoor Switch, and Thermostat. It introduced <a href="http://www.macrumors.com/2016/01/05/ces-2016-idevices-homekit/">four more HomeKit accessories</a> at CES 2016, including a Socket, Wall Switch, Dimmer Switch, and Wall Outlet. The products will be released throughout 2016.<br/>
<br/>
iGrill is an intelligent thermometer that allows you to monitor the temperature of the meat you are grilling on iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch.<br/><br/><div class="linkback">Tags: <a href="http://www.macrumors.com/roundup/idevices/">iDevices</a>, <a href="http://www.macrumors.com/roundup/weber/">Weber</a>, <a href="http://www.macrumors.com/roundup/igrill/">iGrill</a> </div></div>
