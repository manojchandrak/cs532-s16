		<h1 class="entry-title">Guardiola praises beaten Bochum</h1>
				<p class="meta post-meta">Posted on <span class="updated">February 10, 2016</span> by <span class="vcard author"><span class="fn"><a href="http://news.thefantasyfootball.com/author/statzup/" title="Posts by TFF" rel="author">TFF</a></span></span> in <a href="http://news.thefantasyfootball.com/category/news/" rel="category tag">News</a>  //  0 Comments</p>
	</header>
		<div class="entry clearfix">
		
<div class="post-thumbnail">
<img src="http://news.thefantasyfootball.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/arjen-robben-cropped_1iojehw4pox9h1fxpgvbayf0x4jpgt-849930550-620x264.jpeg" alt="Arjen Robben - cropped" title="Guardiola praises beaten Bochum" />
<span class="wp-caption-text">Arjen Robben offers his commiserates (source: Bongarts)</span>
</div>
		
<div class="twitter-share"><a href="https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?url=http%3A%2F%2Fnews.thefantasyfootball.com%2F2016%2F02%2F10%2Fbochum-earned-the-praise-of-pep-guardiola-after-holding-bayern-munich-at-bay-until-going-down-to-10-men-in-the-dfb-pokal-quarter-finals%2F&#038;via=thefantasyfb" class="twitter-share-button">Tweet</a></div>
<p>Pep Guardiola was relieved to come through a &#8220;difficult game&#8221; after Bayern Munich recorded a 3-0 win over second-tier Bochum in the quarter-finals of the DFB-Pokal.</p>
<p>Robert Lewandowski scored twice either side of a goal from Thiago Alcantara as the Bundesliga leaders kept alive their hopes of securing the treble.</p>
<p>However, Guardiola was also quick to praise brave Bochum, who held their heavyweight opponents at bay until the 39th minute.</p>
<p>The 2.Bundesliga side were also forced to play the entire second half with only 10 men following the dismissal of substitute defender Jan Simunek just before the break.</p>
<p>&#8220;We are obviously delighted with the semi-finals. Congratulations to my team,&#8221; Guardiola told the club&#8217;s official website.</p>
<p>&#8220;My compliments also to Bochum. We talked a lot with the players and we knew that it was a difficult game.</p>
<p>&#8220;After the penalty and the red card, we had more control in the second half.&#8221;</p>
<p>Lewandowski &#8211; who broke the deadlock with a low shot before adding Bayern&#8217;s third just before the final whistle &#8211; echoed the sentiments of his manager.</p>
<p>&#8220;Until the first goal, it was not so easy. Bochum played very well,&#8221; he said.</p>
<p>&#8220;In the cup, it is always dangerous as long as the score stands at 0-0.&#8221;</p>
<p>Bayern will host Werder Bremen in the next round of the competition, while the other semi-final sees Borussia Dortmund travel to Hertha Berlin.</p>
