          <div class="intro">
        We'll have to wait a little longer to see Steven Spielberg's sci-fi epic
      </div>
            <ul class="caption meta inline-pipes-list">
              <li class="author" itemprop="author" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <span itemprop="name"><a href="/author/jack-shepherd" title="Jack Shepherd">Jack Shepherd</a></span>
                  </li>
                <li>
          <a href="https://twitter.com/JackJShepherd">@JackJShepherd</a>
        </li>
                    <li title="10 February 2016 11:09 London"><time data-microtimes='{"published":"1455101460000","display":0,"changed":"1455102549000"}' datetime="11:09, 10 February 2016"unixtime="1455102549000">Wednesday 10 February 2016 11:09 BST</time></li>

                    <li class="comments">
          <a href="#commentsDiv" id="comment-count" class="icon-comment"></a>
        </li>
          </ul>
    <div id="gigya-share-btns-1"></div>
  </header>


        <figure itemprop="associatedMedia image" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject" class="featured-media medium-width">
      <meta itemprop="representativeOfPage" content="true">
            <!-- scald=4214266:article_image --><img itemprop="contentUrl" src="http://static.independent.co.uk/s3fs-public/styles/story_large/public/thumbnails/image/2016/02/10/11/Steven-Spielberg.jpg" width="1368" height="907" alt="" /><!-- END scald=4214266 -->              <figcaption itemprop="description" class="caption">
          Steven Spielberg <span class="copyright">Getty</span>        </figcaption>
          </figure>
        
    <div class="article-wrapper clearfix">
      <div class="main-content-column">
      <div class="text-wrapper" itemprop="articleBody">
        <p>With <em>Star Wars Episode VIII</em>’s <a href="http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/films/news/spider-man-pirates-of-the-caribbean-5-and-jumanji-reboot-release-dates-all-changed-due-to-star-wars-a6825016.html" target="_blank">release date</a> changing from May 2017 to 15 December 2017, Hollywood studios has a lot of rearranging to do. All of a sudden, the summer of 2017 was a viable time to release for films, while Christmas 2017 became a no-go zone. </p>

<p>Disney’s <em>Pirates of the Caribbean </em>moved to 26 May, Sony’s <em>Spider-Man</em> to 7 July and <em>Jumanji</em> to the 28 July.</p>

<p>Many were left wondering whether Steven Spielberg would follow suit as <em>Star Wars VIII</em> is now to be released on the same day as his adaptation of <em>Ready Player One</em>.</p>



<p>Warner Bros. has wisely decided to move the film, changing its release date from 15 December 2017, to 30 March 2018. The new release date is the same as a currently-untitled Universal’s Monster franchise movie, likely <em>The Invisible Man </em>which just <a href="http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/films/news/johnny-depp-set-to-play-the-invisible-man-in-universals-classic-monster-universe-a6864731.html" target="_blank">cast Johnny Depp</a> as the lead.</p>

<div class="relatedlinkslist inline-block-related-list inline-block-left">
<h2 class="box-title">Read more</h2>

<div class="relatedlinkslist-content">
<ul class="related"><li><a href="/arts-entertainment/films/news/c-3po-actor-threatens-to-reveal-star-wars-8-plot-director-intervenes-a6851201.html">C-3PO threatens to reveal Star Wars 8 plot, director intervenes</a></li>
	<li><a href="/arts-entertainment/films/news/spider-man-pirates-of-the-caribbean-5-and-jumanji-reboot-release-dates-all-changed-due-to-star-wars-a6825016.html">Release dates of three blockbusters changed due to Star Wars 8 delay</a></li>
	<li><a href="/arts-entertainment/films/news/star-wars-8-secret-working-title-has-supposedly-been-revealed-a6823261.html">Star Wars 8 'secret working title' has supposedly been revealed</a></li>
</ul></div>
</div>

<p>Moving <em>Ready Player One</em> comes with a couple of benefits; it doesn’t have to compete with the sequel to a $2 billion film (<em>The Force Awakens</em>), it can benefit from the following Easter weekend, it falls between the Winter Olympics and World Cup, and sandwiches in-between a DC project and <em>Avengers: Infinity War - Part 1</em>, therefore not competing directly with either.</p>

<p><em>Ready Player One</em> follows the story of a teenager taking part in an online treasure hunt though a virtual word called the Oasis. </p>
