        <p>With cuts facing the UK&rsquo;s junior physicians and a strike taking place today to protest Health Secretary Jeremy Hunt&rsquo;s plans for more weekend working at functionally lower pay, it's time our Doctors stood together &ndash; and apparently that even includes the time-travelling variety.</p>
<p>Yes, Time Lords John Hurt and Peter Capaldi (the War Doctor and the current Doctor, respectively) have thrown their weight behind the junior doctors, donning Vivienne Westwood-designed T-shirts as part of a campaign designed to support the NHS workers.</p>
