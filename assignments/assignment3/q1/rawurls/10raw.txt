					<strong>App World</strong>
					<h1 class="headline" itemprop="headline">YouTuber and Viner Alx James launches his own app</h1>

							<p class="article-body-text content-body-text" itemprop="articleBody">It's the next step for popular YouTubers and Viners.</P><p class="article-body-text content-body-text" itemprop="articleBody">Alx James already has those platforms covered so he decided to launch his own app called "Waterbug Army" for his "waterbugs."</P><p class="article-body-text content-body-text" itemprop="articleBody">For those who aren't familiar with Alx, he calls his fans "waterbugs" as a descriptor for people who wear oversized sunglasses.</P><p class="article-body-text content-body-text" itemprop="articleBody">Alx made the announcement for his app on his YouTube channel today and told Wonderwall.com about why he launched it.</P><p class="article-body-text content-body-text" itemprop="articleBody">"I wanted to create a place where my fans could meet one another. I have millions of people who watch my videos but they only know me! I want them all to get to know each other," he explained. "My fans should not only support me, I want them to support one another! Having a friend, whether through the Internet or in real life, keeps people on the right track."</P><p class="article-body-text content-body-text" itemprop="articleBody"><span class="iframe-wrap is-iframe proxy-iframe content-embed youtube-embed" data-embed-type="youtube" data-embed-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/AyYINxTYWy0">
                <span class="top-left"></span>        
                <noscript><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AyYINxTYWy0">View Video on YouTube</a></noscript>                          
            </span></P><p class="article-body-text content-body-text" itemprop="articleBody">The app will allow his fans to post a variety of different content formats including texts, photos, videos, GIFs and polls as well as comment, repost or remix other user's posts. Fans will also be able to see all of his posts, and he will use the app to give his most passionate following exclusive content and behind-the scenes footage as well as tease upcoming videos.</P><p class="article-body-text content-body-text" itemprop="articleBody">With nearly 700,000 subscribers on YouTube, Alex also has one of the biggest Vine followings, thanks to his over 7 million followers.</P><p class="article-body-text content-body-text" itemprop="articleBody">In 2014, Alx also received his own reality TV deal with Collins Avenue.</P><p class="article-body-text content-body-text" itemprop="articleBody">"Once in a great while a uniquely talented and quick-witted individual like Alx comes along that instantly captures the public's attention," Collins Avenue President Jeff Collins said. "We believe his memorable character spoofs, distinctive voices and extraordinary ability to tell a compelling story with a beginning, middle and end in just six seconds will allow us to expand the bounds of reality television in innovative new ways."</P><p class="article-body-text content-body-text" itemprop="articleBody">And now you can download the app to get even more of his
