			<h1 class="entry-title">Brewers Association Sends American Craft Beer to Peyton Manning</h1> 
			
						  <cite>By CraftBeer.com Staff</cite>
							<time>February 10, 2016</time>
		    			
			<div class="entry">
			  <figure class='featured-image pin-it'><img width="338" height="600" src="http://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/craftbeerdotcom/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/09151451/Peyton_Featured-338x600.jpg" class="attachment-medium size-medium wp-post-image" alt="craft beer for Peyton Manning" itemprop="image primaryImageOfPage" /></figure><p>On Sunday, the Denver Broncos took the Super Bowl 50 title, and Peyton Manning took home his second victory in football&#8217;s biggest game. Congratulations to The Sheriff on a game well played and a win well deserved. And while the feat did indeed earn Mr. Manning the right to kick back with his teammates for a beer or two, the seemingly shameless plug of the World&#8217;s largest beer company made many in craft beer land confused.</p>
<p>Wouldn&#8217;t he rather have a beer more fitting of his superstardom; maybe a beer that more closely represents the Broncos&#8217; status as an underdog? That&#8217;s what we believe small and independent craft brewers represent.</p>
<p>So, the <a href="http://www.BrewersAssociation.org">Brewers Association</a> (publishers of CraftBeer.com) put together a care package for the talented quarterback in hopes of helping him find another post-game refreshment. Since Peyton happens to play in one of the most beer-centric states in the nation, we made sure to include a good number of beers that were local to Colorado in addition to a few other classic and approachable options from around the country.</p>
<p>Along with the beer, we included a <a href="http://craftbeerdotcom.s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/11085440/Peyton_Letter2.pdf" target="_blank">letter of congratulations and appreciation</a> for his hard work. We hope you enjoy the beer, Peyton!!</p>
<a href="http://craftbeerdotcom.s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/10083748/Peyton_internal.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-58619"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-58619" src="http://craftbeerdotcom.s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/10083748/Peyton_internal-1200x675.jpg" alt="beer sent to Peyton Manning" width="1200" height="675" srcset="http://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/craftbeerdotcom/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/10083748/Peyton_internal-600x338.jpg 600w, http://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/craftbeerdotcom/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/10083748/Peyton_internal-768x432.jpg 768w, http://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/craftbeerdotcom/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/10083748/Peyton_internal-1200x675.jpg 1200w" sizes="(max-width: 1200px) 100vw, 1200px" /></a>
<p><strong>Peyton&#8217;s care package included:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Black Bavarian (<a href="http://www.craftbeer.com/styles/german-style-schwarzbier">German-style Schwarzbier)</a> | <a href="http://www.sprecherbrewery.com">Sprecher Brewing Co.</a> | Glendale, WI</li>
<li>Apricot Blonde (<a href="http://www.craftbeer.com/styles/blonde-ale">Blonde Ale)</a> | <a href="http://drydockbrewing.com/">Dry Dock Brewing Co.</a> | Aurora, CO</li>
<li>Craft Lager (<a href="http://www.craftbeer.com/styles/american-lager">American Lager</a>) | <a href="http://upslopebrewing.com">Upslope Brewing Co.</a> | Boulder, CO</li>
<li>Colorado Kolsch (<a href="http://www.craftbeer.com/styles/german-style-kolsch">German-style Kolsch</a>) | Steamworks Brewing Co. | Durango, CO</li>
<li>Racer 5 IPA (<a href="http://www.craftbeer.com/styles/american-india-pale-ale">India Pale Ale</a>) | <a href="http://bearrepublic.com">Bear Republic Brewing Co.</a> | Healdsburg, CA</li>
<li>Samuel Adams Boston Lager (<a href="http://www.craftbeer.com/styles/vienna-style-lager">Vienna-style Lager</a>) | <a href="https://www.samueladams.com">Boston Beer Co.</a> | Boston, MA</li>
<li>Mojo IPA (India Pale Ale) | Boulder Beer Co. | Boulder, CO</li>
<li>Sawtooth Ale Nitro (Extra Special Bitter) | <a href="http://lefthandbrewing.com/">Left Hand Brewing Co.</a> | Longmont, CO</li>
<li>Session Cream Summer Ale (Cream Ale) | <a href="http://www.fullsailbrewing.com/">Full Sail Brewing Co.</a> | Hood River, OR</li>
<li>Graham Cracker Porter (American Porter) | <a href="http://denverbeerco.com">Denver Beer Co.</a> | Denver, CO</li>
</ul>
<p>What American craft beer would you send to Peyton Manning?</p>
