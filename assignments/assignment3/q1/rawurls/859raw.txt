                                                                <div class="windows">
<h2>Windows</h2>
<div class="ie">
<p><strong><span class="internet-explorer">Enabling Cookies in Internet Explorer 9 </span></strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Open the Internet <a id="_GoBack" name="_GoBack"></a>Browser</li>
<li>Click Tools (or &#8220;gear&#8221; icon at top right hand corner) &gt; Internet Options &gt; Privacy &gt; Advanced</li>
<li>Check Override automatic cookie handling</li>
<li>For First-party Cookies and Third-party Cookies click Accept</li>
<li>Click OK and OK</li>
</ol>
<p><strong><span class="internet-explorer">Enabling Cookies in Internet Explorer 10, 11 </span></strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Open the Internet <a id="_GoBack" name="_GoBack"></a>Browser</li>
<li>Click the Tools button, and then click Internet Options.</li>
<li>Click the Privacy tab, and then, under Settings, move the slider to the bottom to allow all cookies, and then click OK.</li>
<li>Click OK</li>
</ol>
</div>
<div class="firefox">
<p><strong><span class="firefox">Enabling Cookies in Firefox</span></strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Open the Firefox browser</li>
<li>Click Tools &gt; Options &gt; Privacy &lt; Use custom settings for history</li>
<li>Check Accept cookies from sites</li>
<li>Check Accept third party cookies</li>
<li>Select Keep until: they expire</li>
<li>Click OK</li>
</ol>
</div>
<div class="chrome">
<p><strong><span class="chrome">Enabling Cookies in Google Chrome</span></strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Open the Google Chrome browser</li>
<li>Click Tools icon Or type in Go to chrome://settings/ to the URL window, hit enter</li>
<li>Click &#8216;Advanced settings&#8217; &gt; Select Privacy &gt; Content settings</li>
<li>Check &#8216;Allow local data to be set (recommended)&#8217;</li>
<li>Click &#8216;Done&#8217;</li>
</ol>
</div>
</div>
<div class="mac">
<h2>Mac</h2>
<div class="firefox">
<p><strong>Enabling Cookies in Firefox</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Open the Firefox browser</li>
<li>Firefox &gt; Preferences</li>
<li>Click &#8216;Privacy&#8217; tab</li>
<li>Under &#8216;History&#8217; select Firefox will: &#8216;Use custom settings for history&#8217;</li>
<li>Check &#8216;Accept cookies from sites&#8217; and then check &#8216;Accept third-party cookies&#8217;</li>
<li>Click OK</li>
</ol>
</div>
<div class="chrome">
<p><strong>Enabling Cookies in Google Chrome</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Open the Google Chrome browser</li>
<li>Chrome &gt; Preferences</li>
<li>Click &#8216;Show advanced settings&#8217; at the bottom.</li>
<li>Under Privacy select &#8216;Content settings&#8217;</li>
<li>Under &#8216;Cookies&#8217; select &#8216;Allow local data to be set (recommended)&#8217;</li>
<li>Click &#8216;OK&#8217;</li>
</ol>
</div>
<div class="safari">
<p><strong>Enabling Cookies in Safari</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>In Safari</li>
<li>Safari &gt; Preferences</li>
<li>Click &#8216;Privacy&#8217; tab</li>
<li>Under &#8216;Block cookies&#8217; check &#8216;Never&#8217;</li>
</ol>
</div>
<div class="mobile">
<p><strong>Enabling Cookies in Mobile Safari (iPhone, iPad)</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Go to the Home screen by pressing the Home button or by unlocking your phone/iPad</li>
<li>Select the Settings icon.</li>
<li>Select Safari from the settings menu.</li>
<li>Select &#8216;accept cookies&#8217; from the safari menu.</li>
<li>Select &#8216;from visited&#8217; from the accept cookies menu.</li>
