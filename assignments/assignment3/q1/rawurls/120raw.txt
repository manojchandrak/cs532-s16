  <article class="ember-view article-content font-body copy-text" data-no-turbolink>
    <p>Former NFL wide receiver Josh Morgan was charged with misdemeanor reckless use of a firearm after accidentally shooting himself, according to <a href="http://www.tmz.com/2016/02/10/ex-49ers-wr-accidentally-shoots-himself-hospitalized/#ixzz3zoJCKv6z">TMZ Sports</a>. </p><p>Morgan reportedly was cleaning the gun when it fired on Jan. 31, per the report.</p><p>Police were called to Morgan&#39;s Virginia residence after responding to a report of a self-inflicted gunshot wound.</p><p>Morgan will reportedly need to appear in court in March. </p><p>The 30-year-old signed with the <a data-in-app-uri="thescore:///nfl/teams/30" href="http://www.thescore.com/nfl/teams/30">New Orleans Saints</a> but was released prior to the 2015 season. Morgan also played for the <a data-in-app-uri="thescore:///nfl/teams/23" href="http://www.thescore.com/nfl/teams/23">San Francisco 49ers</a>, <a data-in-app-uri="thescore:///nfl/teams/20" href="http://www.thescore.com/nfl/teams/20">Washington Redskins</a>, and <a data-in-app-uri="thescore:///nfl/teams/25" href="http://www.thescore.com/nfl/teams/25">Chicago Bears</a>. </p>
