Apparently the GOOD Music fam was in the building including Kim, North West, A$AP Rocky, Tyga and others. The Electric Lady studio session was rich with libations aka beverages and apparently everyone was feeling right.</p>
<p>&#8220;We was having some beverages in there!&#8221; E-Bro said. &#8220;I was really into the music.&#8221;</p>
<p>When speaking about what Kanye wanted to be remembered from the project, he paraphrased Kanye words saying, &#8220;I want you to talk about how amazing the music is, the drums, the bass lines and the song structure.&#8221;</p>
<p>E-Bro also went on to talk about how Kanye had some pretty funny lines on the project. One particular line was about Ray J of all people, who was frequently brought up during the recent Wiz beef.</p>
<p>According to E-Bro Kanye said, &#8220;Me and Ray J would be friends if we wasn&#8217;t in love with the same bitch. You might have hit it first, but I&#8217;m rich though.&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;It&#8217;s not serious its funny,&#8221; E-Bro recalled as he paraphrased rap.</p>
<p>What else will Kanye say? Who knows, but we will be eagerly waiting to hear every word of <del datetime="2016-02-10T23:27:44+00:00">Swish</del>, <del>Waves,</del> T.L.O.P.</p>
