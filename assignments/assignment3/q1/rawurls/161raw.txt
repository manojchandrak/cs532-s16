            <h1 class="entry-title">DAY 1: CLOSED! Abortion centers out of business</h1>
            <section class="entry-meta">
	 <time class="published" datetime="2016-02-10T04:04:11+00:00">February 10, 2016</time>
</section>
          </header>
                      <img width="750" height="422" src="https://40daysforlife.com/media/spring2015roswell4.png" class="attachment-single-featured size-single-featured wp-post-image" alt="spring2015roswell4">                    <div class="entry-content">
                        <p>It all starts today! Prayer, fasting, and peaceful vigil are powerful &#8230; and needed now more than ever in our culture.</p>
<p>Right now, there are babies scheduled to be aborted close to where you live &#8212; and your peaceful presence could be the difference. Find your nearest vigil here:</p>
<p><a href="https://40daysforlife.com/browse-campaigns/" target="_blank">https://40daysforlife.com/browse-campaigns/</a></p>
<p>Moms do turn around at the last moment. For the abortion industry, that means one thing &#8212; lost income.</p>
<p>We start this 40 Days for Life campaign with good news &#8230; as we&#8217;re now aware of TWO more abortion facilities that have CLOSED their doors following 40 Days for Life campaigns!</p>
<p>One was in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma and the other in Roswell, Georgia.</p>
<p>Eight 40 Days for Life vigils were held outside the Oklahoma City center … and there were two in front of the Roswell facility.</p>
<p>This now makes 66 locations &#8230; where people peacefully prayed outside during 40 Days for Life vigils … that have gone out of business.</p>
<p>To God be the glory!</p>
<p>As we praise God for these victories, ask yourself this question:</p>
<p>&#8220;How can God use me to save lives where I live?&#8221;</p>
<p>If you&#8217;ve never been to a 40 Days for Life vigil … you can <a href="https://40daysforlife.com/2016/01/19/episode-2-overcoming-fear/" target="_blank">overcome any natural fear</a> and serve as a peaceful sign of Christ&#8217;s hope &#8212; at the very place where the abortions actually happen.</p>
<p>Pray about going out &#8230; and then find one of the <a href="https://40daysforlife.com/browse-campaigns/" target="_blank">273 locations</a> taking part in 40 Days for Life &#8212; starting TODAY &#8212; and continuing through March 20.</p>
<p><a href="https://40daysforlife.com/media/fall2014okc1.png"><img class="alignright size-full wp-image-8757" src="https://40daysforlife.com/media/fall2014okc1.png" alt="fall2014okc1" width="750" height="300" /></a></p>
