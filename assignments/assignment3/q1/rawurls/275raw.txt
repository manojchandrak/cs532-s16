                                <p class="intro">
                                    Arthur Russell's disco group is the subject of a triple-vinyl set out on Record Store Day.
                                </p>
                                <figure><img src="/images/news/2016/loosejoints-popyourfunk.jpg" border="0" /></figure>
                                <aside class="advert">
                                    <div id="pnlMPU">
	
    <div id='div-gpt-ad-1452773484957-7' class="ad" style='width: 300px; min-height: 250px; padding-bottom: 10px;'></div>

</div>

                                <p style="max-width: 652px">
                                    Loose Joints' <i>Pop Your Funk: The Complete Singles Collection</i> will be released on vinyl via <a href=/record-label.aspx?id=2314>West End Records</a> for Record Store Day.<br /><br />The tracks <a href=/dj/arthurrussell>Arthur Russell</a> created for Loose Joints are among his most enduring, dance floor-ready productions. The group has been the subject of <a href=/news.aspx?id=30206>various rounds</a> of reissues courtesy of West End, but this coming April version is for the completists, taking its basis from a 2013 Japan-only CD set. Included across six sides are six versions of "Is It All Over My Face," among them <a href=/dj/larrylevan>Larry Levan</a>'s original "Female Vocal Version" and two 2001 remixes from <a href=/dj/maw>Masters At Work</a>. The Record Store Day release also includes various takes of the group's other singles, "Pop Your Funk" and "Tell You Today."<br /><br /><u>Tracklist</u><br />A1 Is It All Over My Face (Original 12-inch Version)<br />A2 Pop Your Funk (Original 12-Inch Version)<br />B1 Pop Your Funk (Original Single Vocal Version)<br />B2 Pop Your Funk (Original Single Instrumental Version)<br />B3 Is It All Over My Face (Original 12-Inch Larry Levan Female Vocal Version)<br />C1 Tell You Today (Original 12-Inch Vocal Version)<br />C2 Tell You Today (Original 12-Inch New Shoes Edit)<br />D1 Tell You Today (Original 12 Inch Instrumental Version)<br />D2 Is It All Over My Face (Masters At Work Remix)<br />E1 Is It All Over My Face (MAW Joint Dub)<br />E2 Is It All Over My Face (Unreleased Single Female Vocal Version)<br />F1 Is It All Over My Face (Unreleased Original Full-Length Version)<br /><br /><i>West End Records will release </i>Pop Your Funk: The Complete Singles Collection<i> on April 16th, 2016</i>.<ul class="pb8 list">

</ul><div class=f16><a href="/record-label.aspx?id=2314">Label: West End Records</a></div>
                                </p>
