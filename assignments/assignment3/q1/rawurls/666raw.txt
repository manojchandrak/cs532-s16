<p>You need to have a good idea about the amount of calories you are consuming each day and how much you are burning off through work outs and other physical activities. If you are on a regular work out program, especially in the morning, then you need to develop some <strong><i>healthy lunch ideas for weight loss</i></strong> in the process. Certainly, the post workout meal is the important meal of the day that should include lots of proteins and other minerals that are helpful for your body. Therefore, let us have a look at some of the food items you can consume after a hard workout session at lunch.</p>
<p><b>Lean Meat</b></p>
<p><!--b>

[caption id="attachment_38" align="alignright" width="384"]<a href="http://ifeellight.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/healthy-lunch-ideas-for-weight-loss.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-38 " alt="healthy-lunch-ideas-for-weight-loss" src="http://ifeellight.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/healthy-lunch-ideas-for-weight-loss.jpg" width="384" height="255" /></a> <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/ralphandjenny/6928717092/">Vegetable Plate</a> by Ralph Daily<a href="http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc/2.0/">Attribution-NonCommercial License</a>[/caption]

</b--></p>
<p>Lean meat contains high amount of protein that helps you to boost metabolism and stay full for a long time. While exercising, your body will break down a lot of carbs and protein from your system where the amino acid contents of the lean meat will help to repair the damaged muscles while keeping your body satisfied with the necessary protein needs that it can have during the weight loss process. The low amount of carbs and fats in lean meats is ideal to have a heavy and healthy lunch without the fear of gaining weight.</p>
<p>Lean meats from pork, turkey, chicken are ideal items that can be included in the lunch menu by preparing different meals with these lean meats. Boneless grilled chicken breasts is one of the most healthy and productive meal you can have after a hard work out with weights at the gym which would enable you to burn fat and build muscles at the same time. You can also include egg whites, brown rice, almonds, pistachios and oatmeal in the menu with the lean meats you are consuming.</p>
<p><b>Vegetables</b></p>
<p>The nutritional values are endless in vegetables that will allow you to go through a healthy weight loss process. Having a healthy lunch menu with vegetables will allow you to stay full for a long time with very few calories being consumed. The antioxidants, vitamins, minerals and phytochemicals present in vegetables aids the weight loss and fat burning process to a significant level while also ensuring health benefits with less risk of diseases in the future. Preparing tasty meals for lunch including vegetable items like cucumber, zucchini, lettuce, carrots, onions, beans, broccoli, celery, peas, tomatoes, sweet potato, pumpkin, spinach, corn and so many others will help you shred off the fat from your body in no time.</p>
<p><b>Fish</b></p>
<p>Some fishes contain the same amount of lean protein and mineral like the lean meats mentioned previously. However, the nutritional values of some fished are high and very important to stay healthy during the weight loss process. The omega three fatty acid present mostly in fish oil is very important to keep you cholesterol free and thus reducing the risks of several diseases like heart problems, diabetes and perhaps cancer in the long run. Fishes like sardines, tilapia, salmons, rainbow trout, halibut and sablefish are good source of such nutrition you need to stay healthy.</p>
<p><b>Fruit Salad</b></p>
<p>You can make fruit salads of different types involving all the different healthy natural fruit items that you can find at the grocery stores. Fruits are highly essential in your healthy weight loss process and therefore, making salad with banana, apples, lemon, grapes, berries, oranges and pineapples would help you a lot to stay in a healthy and effective diet plan. You can have these salads as dessert or perhaps late afternoon snack as well helping you to stay full the whole day with less calories being consumed.</p>
<p><b>Black Coffee</b></p>
<p>After a tasty and healthy lunch with combinations of lean meat, fruits and vegetables, it is always ideal to have a cup of black coffee. Caffeine rich items always help a lot in the weight loss process and a cup of coffee will do exactly that. Consuming black coffee after a meal helps to slow down the glucose production in the body through its cholrogenic acid contents and decreasing the production of fat cells thus aiding the fat burning process very effectively.</p>
