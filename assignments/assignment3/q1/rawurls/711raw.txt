      <div class="text-wrapper" itemprop="articleBody">
        <p>Two female suicide bombers blew themselves up in a northeast Nigerian refugee camp, killing at least 56 people.</p>

<p>Some reports say more than 60 people have been killed.</p>

<p>Health and rescue officials say a further 78 people are being treated for wounds in the camp, which houses around 50,000 refugees. </p>

<p>The attack reportedly took place on Tuesday, but a breakdown in the telephone system prevented the information from being made public earlier.</p>

<p>Officials blamed Boko Haram for the bombings at Dikwa, 55 miles northeast of Maiduguri, the biggest city in the region - and birthplace of Boko Haram.</p>

<p>Boko Haram's six-year insurgency has killed around 20,000 people and made around 2.5 million homeless. </p>
