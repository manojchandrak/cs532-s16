    <div class="entry" itemprop="articleBody" id="article-entry">

        
        <p><strong>GLASGOW, Scotland</strong> -- This giant rabbit is in search of his Hoppy Ever After.</p><p>The Scottish SPCA is looking for someone to adopt its continental giant rabbit named Atlas.</p><p>Staff at the center in Cardonald, Glasgow, took in the 7-month-old continental giant rabbit when his owner could no longer look after him, the Scottish SPCA <a href="https://www.scottishspca.org/newsroom/latest-news/giant-rabbit-needs-new-home/" target="_blank">said in a press release</a>.</p><p>"Atlas is already about the size of a westie and is still young with some growing to do," said center manager Anna O'Donnell. "He is a very friendly rabbit who loves attention and getting cuddles."</p><p><a href="https://www.facebook.com/scottishspca/?fref=photo" target="_blank">On Facebook</a>, the SPCA called Atlas a, "larger than life rabbit."</p><p></p><div id="fb-root"></div><script>(function(d, s, id) {  var js, fjs = d.getElementsByTagName(s)[0];  if (d.getElementById(id)) return;  js = d.createElement(s); js.id = id;  js.src = "//connect.facebook.net/en_US/sdk.js#xfbml=1&version=v2.3";  fjs.parentNode.insertBefore(js, fjs);}(document, 'script', 'facebook-jssdk'));</script><div class="fb-post" data-href="https://www.facebook.com/scottishspca/posts/10153217365011876:0" data-width="500"><div class="fb-xfbml-parse-ignore"><blockquote cite="https://www.facebook.com/scottishspca/posts/10153217365011876:0"><p>Our centre in Glasgow is seeking a new home for a larger than life rabbit.The 7 month old continental giant rabbit...</p>Posted by <a href="https://www.facebook.com/scottishspca/">Scottish SPCA</a> on&nbsp;<a href="https://www.facebook.com/scottishspca/posts/10153217365011876:0">Monday, February 8, 2016</a></blockquote></div></div><p>But not just anyone can care for this big bunny.</p><p>O'Donnell said a standard rabbit hutch won't do for Atlas. The new owner will need plenty of space for this growing rabbit.</p><p>They also said they are ideally looking for someone who has kept a continental giant before.</p><p>"We are looking for a specific home for Atlas due to his breed and size," O'Donnell said.</p>
