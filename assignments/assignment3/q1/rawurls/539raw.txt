    <div class="body-copy " itemprop="articleBody">
        <p>Puff Daddy is to host a gala fundraiser at Playboy Mansion, <a href="http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/grammys/6874006/diddy-grammys-city-gala-fundraiser-playboy-mansion" target="_blank">Billboard</a> reports. The event will benefit CITY, short for Community's Inspiring Today's Youth, a non-profit based in Los Angeles that seeks to provide mentorship for young entrepreneurs. The gala is scheduled to be after the Grammys Monday (February 15).</p>
<p>Virgin Group founder Sir Richie Branson is to be the keynote speaker at the CITY gala fundraiser hosted by <a href="http://hiphopdx.com/news/id.36153/title.puff-daddy-on-producing-i-am-the-best">Puff Daddy</a>. On the bill to perform are Kool of Kool &amp; the Gang, Chanel West Coast and DJ Paul Oakenfold.</p>
<p>Other events scheduled to be a part of the gala include a celebrity poker tournament and a fashion show from Hemp Blue.</p>
<p>"We are honored to have such an incredible roster of distinguished guests joining us to raise visibility for these important charitable causes," executive producer Ryan Long says in a statement.</p>
<p>Astronaut Buzz Aldrin and businessman and philanthropist John Paul DeJoria are to receive the Lifetime Achievement Award and Legacy of Inspiration Award, respectively.</p>
