		function combineMenus() {		
			// Insert sub-menu items after the top-level li		
			if(window.widthWithScroll > narrowViewport) {
				submenuItemsAsTopLevelItems();
			}
			
			// Add the secondary menu items to the primary menu and add a class to denote these items came from the secondary menu
			$secondaryMenuObject.find('li').each( function() {
				$mainMenuObject.append($(this).addClass('secondary'));
			});
		}
		function uncombineMenus() {
			// Move the secondary menu items back to the secondary menu
			$mainMenuObject.find('li.secondary').each( function() {

					<div class="page-title"><h1>Cybersmile To Form Part Of A New Twitter Trust And Safety Council	</h1></div>


					<span class="posted-date">
						<time datetime="11th February 2016">11th February 2016</time>
					</span>
					<a href="/wp-content/uploads/image13.jpeg" class="colorbox">
						<img width="1000" height="700" src="/wp-content/uploads/image13.jpeg" class="attachment-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" alt="Cybersmile Cyberbullying Twitter HQ" />					</a>
					<div class="user-generated">
						<h5><span class="orange">Twitter are making an incredible effort to strike the right balance between addressing genuine online abuse while at the same time, empowering and facilitating freedom of speech &#8211; it&#8217;s extraordinarily complex.</span></h5>
<span class="hr"></span>
<p>We are delighted to announce that <a href="/who-we-are">Cybersmile</a> are to make up part of a brand new Trust and Safety Council formed by Twitter. With hundreds of millions of tweets sent per day by 320 million users, the new Twitter Trust &amp; Safety Council will be working hard to strike the perfect balance of <a href="/advice-help/category/social-networks">user safety</a> while protecting Twitter users freedom of expression. Cybersmile and selected experts around the world will be helping Twitter in the formation of new products, policies and programs &#8211; offering the platform a diversity of specialist voices.</p>
<p>As <a href="/news/cybersmile-twitter-in-partnership">trusted safety partners with Twitter</a>, Cybersmile welcome the opportunity to contribute to the growth of Twitter, ensuring <a href="/what-we-do/total-access-support">those users needing extra help and support</a> know <a href="/advice-help">where to find it</a> &#8211; and those abusing freedom of speech, don&#8217;t ruin the experience for the majority of genuine, positive and creative users. With such a huge number of tweets being created each day, Twitter is facing a massive task in addressing this issue &#8211; a commendable one. By compiling such a diverse group to support their efforts, they have certainly made a positive step in the right direction.</p>
<h3 style="text-align: center"><span class="orange">&#8220;Cybersmile have been talking about the need for collective solutions for a long time, to see major stakeholders such as Intel and now Twitter forming action and support groups like this, is very encouraging indeed.&#8221;</span></h3>
<p style="text-align: right"><strong>Dan Raisbeck, Cybersmile Co-Founder</strong></p>
<p>We are thrilled with Twitter&#8217;s continual commitment in addressing <a href="/advice-help/category/what-is-cyberbullying">online abuse</a> and are excited to work closely with everyone involved. If you are affected by online abuse please visit our <a href="/advice-help">Online Abuse and Cyberbullying Help Center</a> or access our <a href="/what-we-do/total-access-support">Total Access Support</a>. For more information about Cybersmile and our work, please explore the following suggestions.</p>

<li><a href="/corporate">Cybersmile Corporate Program</a></li>
<li><a href="/donate">Become a Cybersmile Sustainer</a></li>
</ul>
<span class="hr"></span>
<p>What do you think about Twitter&#8217;s new Trust &amp; Safety Council? Tweet us <a href="https://www.twitter.com/cybersmilehq" target="_blank">@CybersmileHQ</a>!</p>
